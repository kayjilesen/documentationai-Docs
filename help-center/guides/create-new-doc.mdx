## Add a Page

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select your project">
    Click on the desired project from your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add new page">
    Press the "New Page" button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter content">
    Fill in your page using Markdown or rich text.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save & publish">
    Click "Save" and then "Publish" to make it live.
  </Step>
</Steps>
