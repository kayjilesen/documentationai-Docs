## Symptoms

- Edit button disabled
- Changes not saved

## Possible Causes

- Not logged in
- Insufficient permissions

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check login">
    ````bash
    Visit https://kayjilesen.com/login and ensure you are signed in.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review permissions">
    Ask your admin for edit rights if needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>
