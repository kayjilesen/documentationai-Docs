## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info" title="Before you begin">
Ensure you have:
- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
- An email address for account registration
- Project files ready (Markdown, images, or code files)
</Callout>

## Create Your Account

Follow these steps to register and log in to your Kay Jilesen documentation space.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Dashboard" icon="monitor">
    Open your browser and navigate to `https://dashboard.kayjilesen.dev`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Click the **Sign Up** button. Enter your email, create a strong password, and verify your account via email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Return to the login page and enter your credentials. You will land on the main dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Supported Login Methods

Choose your preferred login method.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Email & Password" icon="mail">
    Use your registered email and password for secure access.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Social Login" icon="share-2">
    Connect with Google or GitHub for faster authentication.

    <Callout kind="tip">
      Social login requires granting permissions to access your profile.
    </Callout>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Create a New Documentation Space

Organize your projects with dedicated spaces.

<Steps>
  <Step title="New Space" icon="plus">
    From the dashboard, click **New Space**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Space" icon="settings">
    Enter a name like `My Project Docs`, add a description, and select visibility (public or private).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and Enter" icon="check-circle">
    Click **Create**. You will enter the space editor.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Upload Your First Files

Add content to your space quickly.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Drag and Drop" icon="upload">
    Drag files from your desktop directly into the editor panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse Files" icon="folder">
    Click **Upload** and select Markdown files, images, or ZIP archives.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview Changes" icon="eye">
    Files appear in the sidebar. Click to edit or preview.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Basic Navigation and Editing

Navigate and edit your documentation with ease.

Use the left sidebar for pages and files. The center panel shows the editor with live preview. Top bar has search and publish options.

<CodeGroup tabs="Markdown,HTML">
````markdown
# Welcome to My Project

## Features

- Custom design
- Responsive layout

![Project screenshot](image.png)
````

````html
<!DOCTYPE html>
<html>
<head>
  <title>My Project</title>
</head>
<body>
  <h1>Welcome to My Project</h1>
  <img src="image.png" alt="Project screenshot">
</body>
</html>
````
</CodeGroup>
